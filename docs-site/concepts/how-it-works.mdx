## Ranking

Every app's rank blends three signals, all readable on chain: **token-weighted
votes**, **tag stake**, and **real traffic** (page views, weighted toward
CAPTCHA-verified visits — see [Ad revenue sharing](#ad-revenue-sharing)
below). There's no editorial curation and no paid placement — an app with
more genuine conviction behind it, not a bigger marketing budget, ranks
higher.

## Voting

Anyone can vote for an app by committing NEB to it. A vote's weight is
proportional to the amount committed, and votes feed directly into that
app's rank. Votes aren't spent or burned — they're withdrawable, subject to
the same decaying unstake fee tag stakes use (see below), so a vote is a
real, revocable position rather than a one-time action.

## Tags and staking

Anyone can suggest a tag for an app (`swap`, `nft-marketplace`,
`developer-tools`, …), and anyone can **stake** NEB behind a tag they
believe genuinely describes it. Stake behind a tag:

- Boosts that app's rank.
- Makes the tag itself easier to discover (see the [Group
  map](https://nebulous.world/explore) — tags cluster by shared apps, sized
  by total stake).
- Starts earning a share of that app's ad revenue, proportional to your
  stake relative to everyone else staked on any of that app's tags.

### Withdrawing a vote or tag stake

Both votes and tag stakes can be withdrawn at any time, subject to a
**linearly-decaying unstake fee**: 1% if withdrawn immediately after
staking, decaying linearly to 0% over the following 7 days. The fee
discourages flash-staking purely to farm a moment of rank or revenue share,
without permanently locking anyone's capital.

## Ad revenue sharing

Every app page tracks (privacy-preserving, CAPTCHA-verified) traffic and
serves ads. Aggregate ad earnings are allocated back to individual apps
**proportional to each app's share of revenue-eligible traffic** — visits
that passed bot verification, since only verified traffic should earn
money (this is separate from the *raw* view count that feeds ranking above,
where every visit counts toward visibility).

Once an app's gross revenue is allocated, it's distributed further to
everyone staked behind *any* of that app's tags, proportional to their
stake — so staking early on a tag that turns out to describe a popular app
earns more than staking late, and staking on multiple tags for the same app
doesn't multiply your share beyond your total stake. Earned NEB can be
claimed at any time without withdrawing the underlying vote or stake.

## It's all on chain

Votes, tag stakes, withdrawals, and reward claims are real Solana
transactions against the `nebulous_world` Anchor program — nebulous.world's
UI is a client for that program, not a database with a UI bolted on. See
[the NEB token](/concepts/the-neb-token) for what's actually moving in each
of these transactions.
