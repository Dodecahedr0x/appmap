## Discover what the crowd already knows

[nebulous.world](https://nebulous.world) is a crowd-sourced directory of
apps and products — Solana or otherwise — ranked by real conviction instead
of a listing fee. There's no pay-to-play placement and no opaque algorithm:
every app's rank blends **token-weighted votes**, **tag stake**, and **real
traffic**, and the math behind it is open source.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Ranked by the crowd" icon="chart-simple">
    Votes, tag stake, and traffic feed directly into every app's rank — no
    editorial curation, no paid slots.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Skin in the game" icon="hand-holding-dollar">
    Anyone can suggest a tag for an app. Staking NEB behind a tag you believe
    in boosts that app's rank and puts your conviction on chain.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Upside shared with stakers" icon="chart-line">
    Ad revenue an app's page earns is distributed to everyone staked behind
    it, proportional to stake — across all of that app's tags.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Four steps, all of it on chain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discover">
    Search and filter by tag, chain, or category, sorted by a transparent
    rank score.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Vote">
    Commit NEB to an app you use. Votes are token-weighted and feed straight
    into its rank.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stake a tag">
    Back the tags that actually describe an app — stake boosts its rank and
    starts earning.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn">
    Claim your share of ad revenue anytime, without withdrawing your vote or
    stake.
  </Step>
</Steps>
